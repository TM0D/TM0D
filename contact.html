Contact

If you are looking to get into contact with me on a chatting platform (such as discord), there are a few things to keep in mind. I keep my Discord somewhat locked down, so you will need to be in one of the servers I'm in in order to message me without a friend request. I do not accept unsolicited friend requets from people I do not know. I have DMs on in the Beat Saber Modding Group discord server, Cube Community discord server, and in Grazzy's discord server.

Additionally, I tend not to reply to messages sent out of the blue that only start with "Hi" or "Hello", or "How are you". These are usually indications of scams, and are not worth anyone's time. See nohello.net and dontasktoask.com for more information & examples on how to ask questions that warrent a response.

Available methods of contact include:

  • Bluesky: @saeraphinx.dev
  • Tumblr: @saeraphinx
  • Discord: @saeraphinx
  • Email: contact@saeraphinx.dev

I am most active on my Discord. All other methods of contact are checked less frequently. If you have a question about a project I'm working on, there is high chance that the project has a discord server that you can join to ask questions.
