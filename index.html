Hi, I'm Saera.

I write software, edit videos, and more. I can usually be found working on various projects related to Beat Saber, a VR rhythm game, or working on my various Discord bots.

I am currently the lead editor for Cube Community, where we upload videos of top plays, community highlights, and more. I manage the Noodle Map Monday series, where we showcase the fun and cool stuff the community has put together with Heck's suite of mods. If you want to play the maps yourself, you can find the link to each map in the video description, or you can find a playlist with every map on BeatSaver. This playlist is usually updated within a couple hours of a video coming out.

I've also been involved with managing & developing for projects & events. I developed the voting form & judge's panel for the 2024 BeastSaber Mapping Awards, hosted some matches for the 2024 Beat Saber World Cup, and developed a backend that is in use by the Beat Saber Modding Group for hosting Beat Saber mods. Links to these projects can be found below, or on the projects tab.

If you'd like to get in contact with me, please see my contact page.
